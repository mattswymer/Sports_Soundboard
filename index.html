Welcome to Sports Soundboard

Designed for playing audio at live events — reliable even with poor cell service or crowded networks.

Getting Started

  1. Create sections with "＋ Add New Section". Click a section title to rename it.
  2. Add sounds via Upload (drag-and-drop or the button), Hold-to-Record 🎙️, Text-to-Speech, or YouTube.
  3. Organize by dragging cards between sections.
  4. Customize each card's start time, volume, and fade toggles.

Everything saves locally in your browser — your full board is ready next visit.
