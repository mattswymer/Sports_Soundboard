Welcome to Sports Soundboard!

This app is designed for playing audio at live events directly from your device, ensuring reliability even with poor cell service or crowded networks.

Getting Started

  1. Download First: Before you begin, download all the announcements and music you plan to use onto the computer, tablet, or phone you'll be using at the event.
  2. Add Your Sounds: You have three ways to add audio to the board:
    • Upload: Drag and drop your downloaded audio files directly into the "Announcements" or "Music" columns.
    • Record: Click and hold the üéôÔ∏è button to record a quick announcement directly from your microphone.
    • Text-to-Speech (TTS): Type a phrase, choose a voice, and click "Add" to generate a spoken announcement.
  3. Customize Each Sound: Every sound card has its own controls to set a custom Start Time, adjust the Volume, and toggle Fade In or Fade Out effects.

Good to Know

  • Everything is Saved Locally: All your sounds are automatically saved in your browser, so your complete soundboard will be ready the next time you open the app. Please note that this setup is saved only to the specific device and browser you use and will not transfer to other devices.
  • TTS Voices: The list of available text-to-speech voices is provided by your browser and operating system. The selection may vary on different devices.
